Day 1 - 10:30 A.M. to 2 P.M - MBA department

Snaptastic

Organized By Jhalak

Photography is a story we fail to put in words. Are you a good story teller?
It is an art to tell a story just through pictures. So here we are with Snaptastic- where you have to take a sequence of pictures to narrate a story.
Rules:
1. Team of maximum 3 members per team is allowed.
2. The pictures captured should be within the campus.
3. The participants should be able to explain the story through the pictures.
4. Editing of pictures is allowed.
5. Participants must be able to provide the original photographs.
6. Minimum resolution 5MP.
7. Theme will be disclosed on the day of the event.
8. All members must be college students.
9. Cheaters would be disqualified.

Day 3 - 10:30 A.M. to 2 P.M - MBA department

Design Revolution

Organized By Jhalak

You are now a real graphic designer for your firm You just got instructions from your boss to create a POSTER for an upcoming event or a LOGO for the firm. You will be given all the required information needed for the logo or poster. So bring out the graphic designer in you and show your creativity and designing skills to the world .
Rules:
1. It is an on the spot designing competition.
2. Team of maximum 2 members per team is allowed.
3. Adobe Photoshop, Paint, Illustrator, GIMP, Inkscape, Blender and Corel Draw etc. can be used.
4. Theme will be disclosed on the day of the event.
5. Participants must be able to prove the originality of their work.
6. All members must be college students. 7. Cheaters would be disqualified.
